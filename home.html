Our Event Catering Experience

Since 1972, Shree Sahiram Caterers has been a trusted name in Dehradun, Uttarakhand, for exceptional vegetarian catering. We specialize in creating unforgettable culinary experiences for all your events, big or small. Our passion lies in crafting pure veg delicacies using fresh, local ingredients. We understand the importance of tradition and taste, offering a wide range of delectable vegetarian dishes to tantalize your guests' palates.

Whether you're planning a wedding, a corporate event, or a private gathering, our experienced team will handle everything with care. We offer full-service catering, taking care of menu planning, preparation, and presentation, so you can relax and enjoy your special occasion.
