About Us

Our Story

Shree Sahiram Caterers has been serving delicious vegetarian food with years of experience. It started as a small business in 1972 in Shamli, Uttar Pradesh, . With our commitment to quality and excellence, we have expanded our services and opened a new branch in Dehradun, Uttarakhand.

Our professional catering team is dedicated to serving various events, including weddings, corporate gatherings, birthdays, and more. We offer a wide range of customization options, with over 100+ dishes to choose from, ensuring that your event is a memorable and flavorful experience for all.
